== Description ==
Sell Better with Shop Kit Pro Theme. Shop Kit is the ultimate WordPress WooCommerce theme. The Shop Kit theme is designed to elevate your online shopping experience. Shop Kit WordPress theme brings a new level of customization to WooCommerce-based shops, allowing you to create a unique and visually stunning storefront. Whether you're a fashion, gadget, electronics store, or any other online retailer the Shop Kit WordPress theme is perfect for you. Shop Kit is the perfect solution with a modern and minimalist design with fully responsive layouts. You can choose between stylish 3-column or 4-column grid layouts to showcase your products beautifully. Shop Kit is more than a theme, it's your partner in making your online shop awesome.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
